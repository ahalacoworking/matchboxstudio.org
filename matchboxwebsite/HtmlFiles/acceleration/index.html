The new MatchBOX Acceleration Programs are early-stage, multi-sector programs designed to help founders develop and scale their business ideas. They are intended specifically for regional innovative entrepreneurs solving very focused problems in niche industries. The new programs are headquartered at MatchBOX, and are comprised of two major initiatives: The Entrepreneur Development Program is a twice a year 6-week class supporting the first time entrepreneur. The Venture Development Program is a 12-week once a year class focusing on growing nasacent ventures.
